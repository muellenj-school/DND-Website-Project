Why Balance Matters

Creating something new is only the first step in homebrewing. The real challenge is making sure your ideas fit into the game without overpowering other players or breaking mechanics. Balanced content keeps the game fun, fair, and engaging for everyone at the table. Even small changes to abilities or items can have a big impact during gameplay.

Common Balance Mistakes

  • Giving abilities too many effects at once
  • Making damage output higher than standard classes
  • Ignoring action economy (too many actions per turn)
  • Creating items with no clear limitations
  • Forgetting to compare with official content
  • Quick View: Balance Mistakes
    Too many effects, too much damage, ignoring action economy, no limits.
  • Quick View: Playtesting
    Test often, compare with official content, and adjust slowly.

Playtesting Checklist

Step What to Check Goal
First Test Try the feature in a short encounter See if it works as intended
Mid Test Compare with other players Check fairness and balance
Final Test Use in a full session Ensure it feels fun and natural
Adjustments Change numbers or effects Keep it balanced
